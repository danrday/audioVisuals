Instructions:

You must login to a Spotify account to access the song data needed by this app.

Optional: To save tracks and custom colors, login with your Google account.

Project Goals:

  • Find the variation in a given song's tempo.
  • Use D3.js (Data Driven Documents), a data visualization framework, to chart the variation in a song's tempo.
  • SYNC Button: To sync music with the graphs, press SYNC, which starts a 3-second countdown to zero. At zero, press the play button on the embedded spotify player.
  • Currently Spotify does not allow event listeners on its play button, so this countown sync is a rough workaround. Version 2 should have an auto-sync function.
